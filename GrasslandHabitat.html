The Jurassic Park: Grasslands Page


Details of Grasslands Habitat by National Geographic


Grassland habitats are places that receive more rain than deserts but less precipitation than forests. Most of the plants here are grasses, which don’t need as much water as forest vegetation.

The Kangaroo


Kangaroos also known scientifically as "Macropus rufus" are mammals that live in the grasslands habitat. Their average life span in the wild is up to 23 years, plus they weight up to 200 pounds. They have herbivorous diet in their journey through east Australia.


The Zebra


Zebras also known scientifically as "Equus quagga" are mammals that live in the grasslands habitat. Their average life span in the wild is up to 25 years, plus they weight 440 to 990 pounds. They have herbivorous diet in their journey Around Africa.
